Controls

Easy guide for playing the game

Move: Use W A S D or Arrow keys.

Interact: Press Space when you stand close to an object or a person.

Choices: Use Arrow Up, Arrow Down, and Enter. You can also press 1, 2, or 3.

Goal: Move between rooms, listen carefully, and make the best choice you can.

Rooms: You can go to the kitchen, living room, children's room, and basement.

Tip: Read the task list at the top of the game screen. It tells you what to do next.
