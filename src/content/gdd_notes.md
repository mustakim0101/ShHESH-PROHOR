# GDD Notes

## Title

Shesh Prohor

## Team Role

Teammate B: writing, dialogue, event text, endings, pitch, and GDD support.

## Platform

Browser-based game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## Genre

Narrative survival thriller.

## Controls

- Arrow Left / Right: move
- Arrow Up: enter or descend
- Arrow Down: crouch and hide
- SPACE: interact
- Arrow Up / Down + Enter: select dialogue choices

## Theme Justification

The game is about finding emotional weight in ordinary domestic objects and actions. The apartment, the family, the phone, the radio, and the act of moving from one room to another become the entire structure of tension and care.

## Seven Events

1. Conflicting Signals
Three information sources disagree. The player chooses what to trust.

2. The Question
The younger child asks if everything is okay and begins counting quietly.

3. The Blackout
Power cuts. The player must reach the kitchen and light the candle.

4. The Knock
Someone knocks just as the candle is lit. The older child is unsettled and moving toward the front door.

5. Phone at Zero
If the charger was not found earlier, the phone dies and its loss is permanent.

6. The Empty Bed
The older child's bed is empty. The player must search the basement and reach them in time.

7. Morning Decision
Near dawn, the radio gives one final word. The player makes a last decision shaped by the whole night.
